# ***ACM RECRUITMENT PATTERN HUNT***



The next two terms are: 30 , 42.

The pattern: 2 , 6 , 12 , 20 , 30 , 42.....

This pattern is the multiplication of 2 consecutive numbers.

Explanation: 1\*2=2
